about me

I am a Masters in Computer Science student at UC San Diego. I have 2 years of professional experience in the Software engineering field building and architecting complex, distributed, and data-heavy systems.

I am an incoming Software Engineering Intern at Pinterest for Summer 2022. I currently work as a Graduate Student Researcher in the Bazhenov Lab @ UCSD. My current research is related to loss surfaces and mode connecting volumes in the parameter space of neural networks. I also developed sleep based continual learning algorithms and integrated it with ContinualAi's avalanche. Avalanche recently became a part of the PyTorch ecosystem.

I finished my undergraduate with a Gold Medal in Computer Science & Engineering from PSG College of Technology, Anna University in May 2019. Thereafter, I started my career as a Software Engineer at Arcesium (D. E. Shaw & Co.) and transitioned to a Senior Software Engineer role in the Machine learning department.

My foundation in Java, Python, SQL/Postgres, Javascript and Kubernetes drove multiple scalable and robust multi-tenant applications. I have also worked closely with UX designers and end-users to design products that are highly available, and extensible to onboard new clients. My primary interests include core Software engineering, Machine learning, and Full-stack web development. You can have a detailed look at my professional and academic work here.

People identify me with my love for anime, coffee, animals and travelling. In my spare time, I sketch landscapes, edit videos and play soccer. Additionally, I like reading about topics related to finance. If you want to talk about my projects or meme stocks or want to share pictures of your pets, feel free to reach out to me through email or LinkedIn.
